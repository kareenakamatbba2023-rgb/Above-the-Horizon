The Experience

Evening Journey

5:00 PM — Arrival

The Unveiling

Guests are collected from Radisson Blu Cappadocia by private transfer and arrive at a curated launch field framed by the iconic fairy chimneys. Chilled RedBull Edition cocktails await — served in Salvatore Ferragamo-styled frosted film canister vessels. Staff in minimal black uniforms bear a single emblem. The balloons are already inflating on the horizon.

5:30 PM — Ascent

Lift Off

Guests board their balloon. Custom noise-cancelling headphones are fitted — engraved with their name. A pre-show ambient soundscape begins. No talking necessary. A gentle hum fills the ears as the earth drops away.

6:00 PM — Sunset

The Screening Begins

At golden hour altitude, the film plays. The screen is mounted to the balloon gondola. The real backdrop is the burning sky. Guests sip from curated mid-air tasting kits — three RedBull Edition flavours matched to three acts of the film. Ferragamo-designed glassware and accessories complete the aesthetic.

8:00 PM — Descent

Return

The film ends as stars appear. Slow descent. The soundscape fades to silence. Guests land back — changed.

8:30 PM — Ground

Meet, Dine, Remember

Actor meet-and-greet in an illuminated marquee styled by Salvatore Ferragamo. Followed by a private chef tasting dinner at Radisson Blu Cappadocia — menu designed around the film's setting or narrative, paired with RedBull Edition finishes. The Ferragamo-engraved headphones are theirs to keep.

Experience Enhancements

Ways to go Further

Sensory

Scent Synchronisation

Partner with a perfumer to design a scent for each act of the film — cedar for forests, salt for ocean scenes, petrichor for rain — diffused subtly through the headphone cushion warming element.

Wellness

Pre-Screening Breathwork

A 30-minute guided breathwork session through the headphones as the balloon ascends — priming the nervous system for the sensory silence ahead. Surprising, memorable, on-brand for a premium wellness audience.

Collectible

Film Reel Keepsake

Every guest receives a physical 35mm still from the film in a bespoke branded tin — a tactile memory of a night that was otherwise completely intangible.
